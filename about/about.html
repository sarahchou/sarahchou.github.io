About Me

Hi, I'm Sarah! Here are some fun facts about me.

I was born and raised in San Francisco, California.

I LOVE Taylor Swift! Here is me with a friend at her 1989 World Tour in Santa Clara, California in 2015. I also went to her reputation Stadium Tour in 2017, and hope to attend LoverFest when it gets rescheduled!

I love gymnastics. I was part of Lowell High School's JV and Varsity team, and part of Northeastern's Club Gymnastics team in college. I mostly just do handstands for fun wherever I go nowadays.

I moved to Boston, Massachusetts for college in 2015. I loved showing school spirit for Northeastern University, and even helped plan our homecoming week in 2018 as the Social Media and Marketing chair. Go Huskies!

I recently graduated from Northeastern University with honors with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Cognitive Psychology and a minor in Interaction Design in May 2020.

I am actively searching for roles in software engineering or UX design, or anything that combines the two!
